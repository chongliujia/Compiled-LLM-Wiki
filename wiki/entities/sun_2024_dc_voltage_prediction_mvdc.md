# DC Voltage Prediction based on CNN with Physics Information Embedding for MVDC Distribution Systems

## Summary

This paper proposes a physics-embedded convolutional neural network (PECNN) for terminal dc voltage estimation in MVDC distribution systems. The cited claims describe the PECNN as adding a multi-channel combination layer and a physics operation layer, embedding voltage-current-conductance relationships, and evaluating the method on three MVDC distribution networks with different voltage levels and layouts.
