# A Physics-informed Neural Network Method for LC Parameter Estimation in Three-Phase Inverter

## Summary

This paper proposes a physics-informed neural network (PINN) approach for LC parameter estimation in a three-phase inverter. The cited claims describe the method as combining artificial neural networks with circuit analytical models, estimating DC-link capacitance and AC-side inductance without additional hardware circuitry, and validating the case study with theoretical analysis, simulation, and experimental verification.
